Welcome

Afghan proverbs reflect the deep cultural wisdom of Afghan people. These short, meaningful sayings express values, humor, and life lessons.

تا مرد سخن نګفته باشد عیب و هنرش نهمته باش

"until a man speak his flaws and talents remind hidden"

"Da har chi sabr day, ghata mewa warawee"

"Patience brings the best fruit."
